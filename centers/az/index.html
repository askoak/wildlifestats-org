National Directory · Arizona

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Arizona

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Arizona.

Liberty Wildlife

Phoenix · libertywildlife.org

Nurturing the nature of Arizona through quality wildlife rehabilitation, environmental education, and conservation services for the community.

Southwest Wildlife

Scottsdale · southwestwildlife.org

Rescues Arizona native wild mammals that have lost their homes to development or are found injured in the Sonoran Desert.

Wild At Heart Raptors

Cave Creek · wildatheartraptors.org

Arizona-based raptor rescue organization specializing in owl rehabilitation in the Sonoran Desert region.
