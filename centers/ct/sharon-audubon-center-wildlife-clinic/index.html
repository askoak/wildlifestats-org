Sharon Audubon Center Wildlife Rehabilitation Clinic

National Audubon Society, Inc. (Sharon Audubon Center)

State and federally licensed wildlife rehabilitation facility in Connecticut treating hundreds of sick, injured, and orphaned birds and other wildlife every year with the goal of releasing them back into the wild.
