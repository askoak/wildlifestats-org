National Directory · Illinois

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Illinois

7 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Illinois.

Chicago Bird Collision Monitors

Villa Park · www.birdmonitors.net

Volunteer conservation project dedicated to the protection of migratory birds through rescue, advocacy and outreach in downtown Chicago.

DuPage Wildlife Conservation Center

Glen Ellyn · www.dupageforest.org/places-to-go/centers/wildlife-conservation

Provides care and medical treatment to injured, orphaned, and sick native wild animals with the goal of rehabilitation and release.

Flint Creek Wildlife

Barrington · www.flintcreekwildlife.org

Wildlife rehabilitation and education since 2003, promoting habitat and species conservation.

Fox Valley Wildlife Center

South Elgin

Providing rescue guidance, medical treatment, and rehabilitation for injured and orphaned animals throughout the Fox Valley region.

Illinois Raptor Center

Decatur

Ensures the well-being of native animals through wildlife rehabilitation; increases conservation awareness; contributes expertise to conservation partners.

NIRRE

Loves Park · www.northernillinoisraptor.org

Raptor rescue, rehabilitation, and education in northern Illinois.

TreeHouse Wildlife Center

Dow · www.treehousewildlifecenter.com

501(c)(3) nonprofit established in 1979 dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of native wildlife while promoting environmental awareness through education.
