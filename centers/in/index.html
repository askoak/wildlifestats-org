Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Indiana

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Indiana.

Munster · www.humaneindiana.org

Wildlife Rehabilitation and Education Center preserves and protects native wildlife from 26 Indiana counties.

Indiana Raptor Center

Nashville · www.indianaraptorcenter.com

A hospital for birds of prey in the hills of Brown County, Indiana.

Re-Wilding Indiana

Greenfield · www.rewildingindiana.org

Providing guidance and rescue help for orphaned, injured, or ill wildlife and giving them the care they need to return to the wild.
