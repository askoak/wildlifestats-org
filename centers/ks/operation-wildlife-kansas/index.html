Operation Wildlife (OWL)

Operation Wildlife, Inc.

Provides rehabilitation and veterinary services to injured and orphaned wild animals in the Kansas City metro and surrounding communities in northeast Kansas.

EIN: 48-1078633

Form 990 financial data for this organization will be available here once the Form 990 ingestion pipeline (Phase 8b) ships. In the meantime, the canonical source for this organization's tax filings is ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer.

Notes

Founded 1989. Largest publicly funded wildlife rehabilitation in Kansas per KDWPT statistics. 4,000-sq-ft facility with surgical suite, laboratory, isolation room, flight pens, outdoor enclosures. Release rate averages 69% (vs 49% national average). Annual revenue ~$540K. Covers 5,000 sq miles in NE Kansas. Tax-exempt since April 1994.
