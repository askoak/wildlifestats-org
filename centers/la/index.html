Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Louisiana

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Louisiana.

Acadiana Wildlife

Youngsville · acadianawildlife.org

Wildlife education and rehabilitation nonprofit serving the Acadiana / Lafayette Parish region of Louisiana.

Clearwater Sanctuary

Covington · clearwatersanctuary.org

501(c)(3) sanctuary on 100 acres rehabilitating injured and orphaned Louisiana wildlife. In service since 1989.

WERLA

Shreveport · werla.net

Rehabilitation of injured and orphaned native wildlife and education about wildlife in northwest Louisiana.
