Center for Wildlife

Center For Wildlife, Inc.

Strengthening our community's overall health through conservation medicine and nature-based education. For 39 years, treated over 90,000 injured and orphaned wild animals representing 190+ species. Does NOT accept rabies vector species (fox, raccoon, skunk, bat, groundhog).
