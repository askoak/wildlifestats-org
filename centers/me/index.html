National Directory · Maine

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Maine

2 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Maine.

Avian Haven

Freedom · avianhaven.org

Bird-only wildlife rehabilitation facility in Freedom, Maine. Accepts all birds and reptiles; has multiple flight cages. Operates on North Palermo Road in Waldo County.

Center for Wildlife

Cape Neddick · www.thecenterforwildlife.org

Strengthening our community's overall health through conservation medicine and nature-based education. For 39 years, treated over 90,000 injured and orphaned wild animals representing 190+ species. Does NOT accept rabies vector species (fox, raccoon, skunk, bat, groundhog).
