National Directory · Missouri

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Missouri

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Missouri.

Endangered Wolf Center

Eureka · www.endangeredwolfcenter.org

Breeding, conservation, and education for critically endangered wolves; every Mexican gray wolf in the wild traces lineage to this center.

Wildlife Rescue Center

Ballwin · www.mowildlife.org

Rehabilitates injured, sick and orphaned native wildlife and releases healthy animals to their natural habitat while providing environmental awareness through educational outreach.

World Bird Sanctuary

Valley Park · www.worldbirdsanctuary.org

Founded in 1977 to provide the St. Louis area with a hospital for sick or injured raptors; now internationally renowned raptor rehab and education center on 305 acres.
