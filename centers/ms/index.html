Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Mississippi

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Mississippi.

Mississippi Wildlife Rehabilitation

Coldwater · mswildliferehab.org

Founded 1995 to meet the growing need for wildlife assistance in north Mississippi. Education programs for schools and civic orgs.

Wild Again in Mississippi

Enid · www.wildagaininmississippi.org

Wildlife rescue and rehabilitation serving Mississippi with focus on native mammals.

Woodside Wildlife Rescue

Pass Christian · woodsidewildliferescue.org

Dedicated to rescue and rehabilitation of Mississippi's wildlife and educating the community on coexistence with wildlife.
