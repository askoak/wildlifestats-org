Notes

Raptor Recovery is a program of Fontenelle Forest (founded 1913). One of Nebraska's oldest conservation organizations; National Natural Landmark. 130+ statewide volunteers. Saves ~600 raptors/year in Nebraska and western Iowa. 25 non-releasable birds of prey as educational ambassadors. Wildlife Rescue Team program director: Denise Lewis. Refer non-raptor birds to Nebraska Wildlife Rehab (402-234-2473).
