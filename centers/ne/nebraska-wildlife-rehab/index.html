Nebraska Wildlife Rehab

Nebraska Wildlife Rehab, Inc.

To rehabilitate native Nebraska wildlife and migratory birds for return to the wild, to preserve and restore native habitats, and to educate youth and adults about the importance of wildlife and ecosystems.

Notes

Founded 1999. Operates under permits from Nebraska Game & Parks Commission and U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service. Over 8,000 patients in 2024 from 174 species. Annual operating budget ~$1.7M. Offers after-school programming in 4 middle schools; 15 summer university internships; High School Science Academy. 25,000+ community wildlife inquiries/year.
