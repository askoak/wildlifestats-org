Raptor Conservation Alliance

Raptor Conservation Alliance Inc.

To provide care and support for injured and orphaned raptors in preparation for release; to assist in the management of raptor populations and raptor research; to enlist public support for raptor conservation through educational programs.

Notes

Successor to Raptor Recovery Nebraska (founded 1976), merged with Nature's Educators (Colorado). Over 14,000–15,000 raptors admitted in 45+ years — one of top 10 raptor rehab organizations nationally. ~50% release rate. Statewide volunteer transport network. Maintains non-releasable raptors as educational ambassadors. Also serves western Iowa.
