Elaine Conners Wildcare

Elaine Conners Center for Wildlife, Inc.

Wildlife rehabilitation center providing professional and compassionate care to injured and orphaned native wildlife in the Mount Washington Valley area. Gated facility; priority to Carroll and Coos Counties. Open 9am–6pm daily.
