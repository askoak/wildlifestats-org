Green Chimneys Wildlife Rehabilitation Center

Green Chimneys School

Paul C. Kupchok Wildlife Rehabilitation Center provides rescue, rehabilitation, and continued care for injured, orphaned, and distressed wildlife, primarily birds of prey, at the Green Chimneys campus in Brewster.
