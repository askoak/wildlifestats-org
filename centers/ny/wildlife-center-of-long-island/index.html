Wildlife Center of Long Island

Wildlife Center of Long Island, Inc.

Dedicated to the preservation of Long Island's wildlife and natural habitats since 1982. Primary resource for injured and orphaned wildlife on Long Island, serving Nassau and Western Suffolk Counties.
