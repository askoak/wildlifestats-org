Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Ohio

5 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Ohio.

Lake Erie Nature & Science Center

Bay Village · www.lensc.org

Educate and inspire people of all ages to value and protect the natural world.

Medina Raptor Center

Spencer · www.medinaraptorcenter.org

Dedicated to the care and rehabilitation of sick, injured, or orphaned birds of prey in Northeast Ohio.

Ohio Bird Sanctuary

Mansfield · www.ohiobirdsanctuary.com

Wildlife rehabilitation and conservation education focusing on native Ohio birds.

Ohio Wildlife Center

Powell · www.ohiowildlifecenter.org

Ohio Wildlife Center is dedicated to fostering awareness and appreciation of Ohio's native wildlife through rehabilitation, education and wildlife health studies.

RAPTOR Inc.

Milford · raptorinc.org

Education and rehabilitation of raptors in the Cincinnati/Clermont County region of Ohio.
