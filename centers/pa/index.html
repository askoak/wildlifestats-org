National Directory · Pennsylvania

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Pennsylvania

5 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Pennsylvania.

Aark

Chalfont · www.aark.org

Non-profit wildlife center dedicated to the rehabilitation and release of orphaned and injured Pennsylvania wildlife and promoting harmony between humans and their wild neighbors through quality environmental education.

Philadelphia Metro Wildlife Center

Norristown · www.phillywildlife.org

Hospital run by licensed wildlife rehabilitation professionals treating all species of injured or orphaned wildlife in the Philadelphia metropolitan area.

Pocono Wildlife / The Wilderz

Stroudsburg · poconowildlife.com

PA Certified & Licensed Wildlife Rehabilitation Center dedicated to making a positive change by promoting humane and compassionate treatment of all living species. Serves Monroe County and the Pocono region.

Raven Ridge Wildlife Center

Washington Boro · ravenridgewildlifecenter.org

Non-profit wildlife rehabilitation center in Lancaster County specializing in healing injured birds of prey, mammals, and waterfowl. Opened 2015.

Schuylkill Wildlife Clinic

Philadelphia · schuylkillcenter.org/wildlife-clinic

The only licensed wildlife rehabilitation clinic in the city of Philadelphia. Acts as an emergency room and hospital for thousands of wild animals. Part of the Schuylkill Center for Environmental Education.
