National Directory · Rhode Island

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Rhode Island

2 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Rhode Island.

Congress of the Birds

Providence · www.congressofthebirds.org

To rehabilitate every wild bird in need, and establish a compassionate environment for wild birds through advocacy, education, and art. Only avian-specific rehabilitation facility in Rhode Island with an onsite release center.

Wildlife Clinic of Rhode Island

Saunderstown · www.riwildliferehab.org

Providing direct, hands-on medical and rehabilitative care to ALL species of injured or orphaned wildlife in Rhode Island since 1993. The only non-profit in RI licensed to provide veterinary and rehabilitative care to all wildlife species.
