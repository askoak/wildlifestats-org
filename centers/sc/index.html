Wildlife rehabilitation centers in South Carolina

3 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in South Carolina.

Carolina Wildlife Center

Columbia · carolinawildlife.org

Founded 1989. Devoted to rehabilitation of injured and orphaned native wildlife and preservation of wildlife through education.

Center for Birds of Prey

Awendaw · thecenterforbirdsofprey.org

Providing medical care for injured birds of prey and shorebirds; education, research, and conservation. 15,000+ cases since founding.

Wildlife Rehab Group SC

Greenville · wildlife-rehab.com

Animal rehabilitation organization in the Upstate South Carolina region.
