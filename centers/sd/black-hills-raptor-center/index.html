Notes

SD's principal raptor rehabilitation and education nonprofit, operating since at least 2010 (990-EZ filing history). All-volunteer group managed by 8-person board. Currently building a permanent public education facility in western SD. Conducts 125–150 educational programs per year using live raptors. SD GF&P has no public rehabber directory; BHRC is the most prominent formally incorporated raptor rehab org in the state. EIN confirmed via ProPublica (https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/274423598).
