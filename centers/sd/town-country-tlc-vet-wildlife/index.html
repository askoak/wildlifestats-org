Town & Country TLC Vet (wildlife rehabilitation program)

Accepts ill, injured, or orphaned native wild animals (with exceptions); primary goal is rehabilitation and release back to the wild.

Notes

Veterinary clinic in Rapid City, SD that operates a licensed wildlife rehabilitation program accepting native wild animals. Not a standalone nonprofit — a private veterinary practice. Included here as one of SD's very few publicly listed intake points for injured wildlife. SD GF&P directs the public to call regional wildlife offices because "South Dakota has very few licensed wildlife rehabilitators." This clinic is referenced on SD-facing bird-rescue directories. EIN not applicable (for-profit veterinary practice, not a 501(c)(3)).
