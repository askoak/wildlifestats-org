Last Chance Forever, The Bird of Prey Conservancy

Notes

Founded 1978 by Master Falconer and Vet Tech John Karger. Raptors only (hawks, owls, falcons, eagles, vultures). Primary care facility in NE San Antonio houses 80–100 birds at any given time. 62%–80% release rate. Over 200 education programs/year reaching 500,000+ people annually. 501(c)(3); EIN not publicly indexed in standard sources.
