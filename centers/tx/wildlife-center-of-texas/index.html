Wildlife Center of Texas (Houston SPCA affiliate)

To rescue, rehabilitate, and release native wildlife and to provide sanctuary, individualized care, and a voice for other animals in need.

Notes

Affiliate of Houston SPCA operating within Houston SPCA main campus at 7007 Old Katy Rd. Accepts oiled wildlife; specialty oiled wildlife response. The Wildlife Center of Texas brand is now used for the SPCA's wildlife program separate from Wildlife Rescue & Rehabilitation (Kendalia). TPWD lists both as distinct permitted entities for Harris County.
