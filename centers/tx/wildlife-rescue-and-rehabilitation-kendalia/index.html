Notes

Founded 1977. 212-acre facility in Texas Hill Country near Austin and San Antonio. Also provides permanent sanctuary for ~600 resident animals including non-native and farmed animals. The Wildlife Center of Texas (Houston) is a separate affiliate org at Houston SPCA. San Antonio intake location (11902 Rustic Ln) consolidated to Kendalia as of Oct 21, 2025. Charity Navigator rated.
