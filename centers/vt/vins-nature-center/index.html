Notes

Vermont's only institutional-scale wild bird rehabilitation center. Treats all birds including raptors, waterfowl, songbirds, gamebirds, and threatened/endangered species from throughout New England. Listed on the Vermont F&W licensed rehabber directory as the primary statewide bird rehab intake point. Tax-exempt since August 1973; revenue ~$3M annually. 35,000+ visitors per year. ADA accessible. Adopt-a-Raptor (RISE) program. EIN confirmed via ProPublica (https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/30231665).
