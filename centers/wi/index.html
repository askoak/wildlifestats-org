National Directory · Wisconsin

Wildlife rehabilitation centers in Wisconsin

4 verified licensed wildlife rehabilitation organizations in Wisconsin.

DCHS Wildlife Center

Madison · www.giveshelter.org/wildlife-center

Wildlife Center of Dane County Humane Society, formerly Four Lakes Wildlife Center, founded 2002 to provide wildlife rehabilitation in the Madison area.

Hoo's Woods Raptor Center

Milton · hooswoods.org

Help birds of prey through education, rehabilitation and conservation.

REGI

Antigo · www.raptoreducationgroup.org

Dedicated to the care and rehabilitation of sick, injured, or orphaned native bird species and public education of wildlife issues.

Wisconsin Humane Society Wildlife Rehabilitation Center

Milwaukee · www.wihumane.org/services/wildlife

Care for more than 150 different species of animals, including all species of native birds, reptiles, and amphibians, as well as most mammals.
