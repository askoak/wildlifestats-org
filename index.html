WildlifeStats · In development

A national research framework for wildlife rehabilitation data.

WildlifeStats will turn the patient records of America's wildlife rehabilitation centers into a public knowledge layer for researchers, educators, journalists, conservation organizations, and citizen scientists.

The platform is in design. The first deliverables will include a national admissions dashboard, a One Health hub, a species encyclopedia, a national-parks lens, and a searchable database of public-tier wildlife records.

Standardized intake and outcome data — ingested from heterogeneous center systems, AI-cleaned, privacy-disciplined, and aggregated to the public tier — will let anyone ask the questions wildlife rehabilitation has been quietly answering for decades.
