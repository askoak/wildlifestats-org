One Health

One Health hub

The One Health hub examines the connections among wildlife, domestic animals, and human health. Wildlife rehabilitation data is an early indicator for several diseases of shared concern — among them highly pathogenic avian influenza, leptospirosis, rabies, and West Nile virus — and the pattern of admissions across regions and seasons can reveal how these pressures move through the landscape. This hub will present cross-species disease patterns and regional zoonotic-risk views drawn from the national dataset, framed for researchers and public-health audiences rather than clinical diagnosis.
