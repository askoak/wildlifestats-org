National Parks

National Parks lens

The National Parks lens views the dataset through proximity to National Park Service units. Each park receives a one-page profile drawn from the counties surrounding it: the species most often admitted nearby, the dominant reasons for admission, and the seasonal shape of intake. The lens is educational and is built on the synthetic dataset, so the figures are plausibly shaped by region rather than tied to confirmed events within any park. It is intended to make national patterns legible through a familiar geographic frame.
