Wildlife

Wildlife encyclopedia

The wildlife encyclopedia organizes the dataset by taxonomy. Visitors browse from broad class — mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, marine species — down through ecological guild, such as raptors, songbirds, or sea turtles, to individual species. Each species page will carry a range view, the intake reasons most associated with it nationally, its seasonal pattern of admission, and concise guidance on what to do if you encounter one. The encyclopedia turns the national dataset into a reference that can be read species by species.
